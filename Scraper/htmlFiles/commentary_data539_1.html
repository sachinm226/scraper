Hello and welcome to Cricinfo's live coverage of the first Twenty20 international between South Africa and Australia being played in Johannesburg. The Tests are over, and the focus now shifts to the limited-overs format and both teams will be looking for some handy preparation ahead of the IPL - for those who are involved - and the World Twenty20 scheduled for June in England.

Johan Botha, who led the Twenty20 and ODI side well in Australia, will captain South Africa. There's no Peter Siddle or Ben Hilfenhaus for the visitors - both have gone home - so Johnson and limited-overs specialist Nathan Bracken could lead the attack.

There's still a while before the start, so read Peter English's preview to the game in the meantime. We'll get you the toss result and the teams shortly.

The news from the Wanderers is that South Africa have won the toss and opted to field.

Here are the teams:

South Africa: Gibbs, HM Amla, AB de Villiers, JP Duminy, VB van Jaarsveld, MV Boucher, JA Morkel, J Botha, RJ Peterson, WD Parnell, DW Steyn .

So Herschelle Gibbs, Robyn Peterson and Wayne Parnell are the new inclusions. They didn't play a part in the Tests.

Australia: Warner, BJ Haddin, RT Ponting, CL White, MEK Hussey, DJ Hussey, MJ Clarke, JR Hopes, SM Harwood, MG Johnson, B Geeves

Three new names for Australia as well: Brett Geeves, David Hussey and fast bowler Shane Harwood.

Michael Clarke will open with David Warner, who is facing Dale Steyn. All set.
